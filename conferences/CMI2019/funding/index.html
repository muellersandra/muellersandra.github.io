Funding

Support for travel and accommodation is available. Graduate students, young researchers, female mathematicians and members of underrepresented groups are particularly encouraged to apply.

If you want to apply for funding, please send an e-mail to the organizers including a statement about yourself (approx. 1-2 pages) in which you address the following points.
  1. Please provide a short research description. In case you are a graduate student, a short description of the project you are planning to work on suffices here.
  2. What is your prior knowledge in inner model theory?
  3. Why are you interested in attending this workshop?
  4. How are you planning to apply the techniques you want to learn during this workshop in future research projects?
  5. Finally, please give an estimate of your travel costs.
The deadline for funding applications is April 15, 2019. We will inform you about the outcome of your application shortly afterwards.
